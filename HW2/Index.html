About Me

I grew up in Central Kenya, in a small town called Maragua. I went to elementary and Secondary school near the town. After high school, I joined an accounting college in the Nairobi. I worked for a couple of years as an accountant then emigrated to USA in 2004. During my earlier years, I loved playing soccer with my brothers and friends, we participated in youth soccer in the district. I played soccer after high school as a goal keeper in locally organized league. I was active in athletics in high school, I run mostly 800M, 1500M and 5000M races. I also participated in long jump and triple jump. I still hold the district record in triple jump, which I set during District Athletics Meeting in my senior year.

After moving to Iowa City, I was blessed with two daughters aged 9 and 6. I attended Kirkwood Community College for my AA degree.I am a student at University of Iowa studying Finance and MIS graduating in December 2012. I hope to work in Finance or Accounting after I graduate.

I have been back to Kenya four times since 2004 to see my family. My parents, brothers and sisters live in Kenya

I'm big soccer fan, especially English premier league. My team is Manchester United "Go Red Devils". I love watching sports, all kinds of sports.
